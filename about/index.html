Hello, World!

As you most likely know by now, my name is Marco Zamora. I am a web developer adept at both front- and back-end development. I began my journey into web development by taking online classes for fun.

Most notably was the Introduction to Computer Science course available for free from Harvard University. After the first class, I was hooked. Not too long after, I enrolled in a postbaccalaureate program at Oregon State University where I earned a B.S. in Computer Science. In school and in my spare time, I have gained experience planning and executing responsive websites.

My websites ordinarily require me to work with:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • Bootstrap
  • MongoDB

I choose projects with the goal of learning a new skill or even refining my skills with libraries and frameworks I have used in the past. It's a rewarding experience to discover new ways to solve reoccuring situations.

I kept this page free from clutter, so I hope you will check out some of my projects to see the fun I have with working with some JavaScript libraries!
